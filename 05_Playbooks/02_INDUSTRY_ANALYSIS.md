# Industry Analysis Playbook

## Purpose

The Industry Analysis Playbook defines the standardized process for understanding the industry in which a company operates.

Even an excellent company can become a poor investment if it operates in a structurally weak industry.

Understanding industry dynamics provides essential context before evaluating financial performance or valuation.

---

# Objective

Answer one fundamental question:

"Is this industry attractive for long-term investment?"

---

# Required Inputs

- Industry Reports
- Market Research
- Competitor Analysis
- Government Publications
- Annual Reports
- Earnings Calls

---

# Key Questions

## Industry Overview

- What industry does the company operate in?
- What problems does the industry solve?
- How large is the market?
- Is the market expanding or shrinking?

---

## Industry Structure

- How many major competitors exist?
- Is the industry fragmented or concentrated?
- Who are the market leaders?

---

## Growth Drivers

- Technology
- Demographics
- Regulation
- Consumer Behavior
- Economic Growth

---

## Competitive Forces

Evaluate using Porter's Five Forces:

- Threat of New Entrants
- Bargaining Power of Suppliers
- Bargaining Power of Customers
- Threat of Substitutes
- Competitive Rivalry

---

## Industry Economics

Assess:

- Average Profit Margin
- Capital Intensity
- Pricing Power
- Switching Costs
- Innovation Rate

---

## Industry Risks

- Regulation
- Disruption
- Cyclicality
- Commodities
- Geopolitics

---

# Expected Output

Produce:

- Industry Summary
- Industry Growth Outlook
- Competitive Landscape
- Key Opportunities
- Major Risks
- Overall Industry Attractiveness

---

# Confidence Assessment

High

Industry structure is well understood.

Medium

Some structural factors remain uncertain.

Low

Industry outlook cannot yet be confidently assessed.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Ignoring industry lifecycle.
- Confusing company quality with industry quality.
- Ignoring structural risks.
- Overestimating short-term trends.

---

# Definition of Done

Industry Analysis is complete only if:

✓ Industry can be explained clearly.
✓ Market size is understood.
✓ Growth drivers are identified.
✓ Competitive forces are assessed.
✓ Key risks are documented.
✓ Industry attractiveness is justified with evidence.

---

# Philosophy

Great companies usually emerge from great industries.

Understanding the industry helps explain why companies succeed or fail.
