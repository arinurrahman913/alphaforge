# Quality Control Framework

## Purpose

The Quality Control Framework ensures that every investment analysis produced by AlphaForge meets consistent standards of accuracy, transparency, and reasoning quality.

Quality control is performed before any investment thesis is considered complete.

---

# Objective

Answer one fundamental question:

"Can this investment thesis be trusted?"

---

# Quality Standards

Every investment thesis must satisfy the following standards.

## Completeness

Have all required playbooks been completed?

- Company Analysis
- Industry Analysis
- Competitive Analysis
- Financial Analysis
- Risk Analysis
- Valuation Analysis

---

## Evidence

Are conclusions supported by reliable evidence?

Examples:

- Annual Reports
- Financial Statements
- Earnings Calls
- Industry Reports
- Regulatory Filings

---

## Reasoning

Does every conclusion logically follow from the evidence?

Avoid:

- Unsupported assumptions
- Emotional language
- Confirmation bias

---

## Consistency

Are conclusions consistent across all analyses?

Example:

A company cannot simultaneously be described as:

- High Risk

and

- Extremely Stable

without explanation.

---

## Explainability

Can another analyst understand how the conclusion was reached?

Every major conclusion should be explainable.

---

# Quality Assessment

Rate overall quality as:

- Excellent
- Good
- Needs Improvement

---

# Definition of Done

Quality Control is complete only if:

✓ All analyses are completed.

✓ Evidence supports conclusions.

✓ No major inconsistencies remain.

✓ Risks are acknowledged.

✓ Reasoning is transparent.

✓ Investment thesis is reproducible.

---

# Philosophy

High-quality decisions come from high-quality reasoning.

Quality should be measured by process, not outcome.
