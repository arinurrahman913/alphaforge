# AlphaForge Reasoning Template

## Purpose

The Reasoning Template defines the standard thinking process used by AlphaForge before producing any conclusion.

Every conclusion must be explainable.

Reasoning is mandatory.

---

# Standard Reasoning Flow

Question

↓

Context

↓

Evidence

↓

Analysis

↓

Reasoning

↓

Risk

↓

Alternative Views

↓

Conclusion

↓

Confidence

---

# Standard Output

Every analysis should answer:

## Question

What problem are we trying to solve?

---

## Context

What is happening?

---

## Evidence

What facts support the analysis?

---

## Analysis

What does the evidence indicate?

---

## Reasoning

Why does the evidence lead to this conclusion?

---

## Risks

What could make this conclusion wrong?

---

## Alternative Views

What other reasonable interpretations exist?

---

## Conclusion

State the final conclusion clearly.

---

## Confidence

Rate confidence:

- High
- Medium
- Low

Confidence should reflect evidence quality rather than certainty.

---

# Philosophy

Never skip reasoning.

Never jump directly to conclusions.

Transparent reasoning builds trust.
