# AlphaForge Playbooks

## Purpose

Playbooks define how AlphaForge thinks.

They are not prompts.

They are not templates.

They are standardized reasoning frameworks that guide every investment analysis.

Every AI agent, analyst, or future contributor should follow the same reasoning process to ensure consistency, transparency, and explainability.

---

# Philosophy

Good investment decisions are built through disciplined reasoning.

AlphaForge does not generate conclusions first.

AlphaForge builds conclusions from evidence.

Every playbook exists to improve decision quality.

---

# Design Principles

- Evidence before opinion.
- Reasoning before conclusion.
- Consistency over intuition.
- Explain every decision.
- Every conclusion must be supported by evidence.
- Every analysis should be reproducible.

---

# Standard Playbook Structure

Every playbook follows the same structure.

1. Purpose
2. Required Inputs
3. Key Questions
4. Required Evidence
5. Reasoning Process
6. Expected Output
7. Confidence Assessment
8. Common Mistakes
9. References

# Goal

Playbooks transform knowledge into structured reasoning.

Knowledge answers:

"What do we know?"

Playbooks answer:

"How should we think?"

---

# Long-Term Vision

As AlphaForge evolves, playbooks will become the institutional reasoning standard that powers every AI agent and investment decision.

Technology may change.

Reasoning standards should endure.
