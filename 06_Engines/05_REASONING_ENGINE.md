# Reasoning Engine

> Transforming verified evidence into structured investment reasoning.

---

## Purpose

The Reasoning Engine is responsible for transforming validated investment evidence into logical, structured, and explainable investment reasoning.

Rather than predicting outcomes, the Reasoning Engine applies AlphaForge's investment philosophy, frameworks, and playbooks to interpret evidence objectively.

The Reasoning Engine never makes the final investment decision.

It produces reasoning.

---

## Mission

Generate transparent, consistent, and explainable investment reasoning that forms the foundation of every AlphaForge investment decision.

---

## Philosophy

Reasoning should always be based on evidence.

Every conclusion must be supported by verifiable facts.

Investment reasoning should be:

- Logical
- Explainable
- Consistent
- Repeatable
- Evidence-Based

Reasoning is not prediction.

Reasoning is structured interpretation.

---

## Primary Objective

Transform Evidence Packages into structured Reasoning Packages using AlphaForge's investment methodologies.

---

## Responsibilities

The Reasoning Engine is responsible for:

- Applying AlphaForge Frameworks
- Applying AlphaForge Playbooks
- Connecting evidence logically
- Identifying strengths
- Identifying weaknesses
- Identifying opportunities
- Identifying risks
- Generating investment reasoning
- Producing standardized Reasoning Packages

## Success Criteria

The Engine succeeds when:

- Every conclusion is supported by evidence.
- Frameworks are applied correctly.
- Reasoning is explainable.
- Reasoning is reproducible.
- Contradictory evidence is addressed.
- Investment logic is complete.

---

## Failure Conditions

The Engine must reject processing if:

- Evidence Package is incomplete.
- Critical evidence is missing.
- Framework execution fails.
- Reasoning cannot be supported by evidence.
- Significant logical conflicts remain unresolved.

---

## Design Principles

- Evidence before Conclusion
- Logic before Opinion
- Explainability First
- Framework Driven
- Playbook Oriented
- Consistency Always
