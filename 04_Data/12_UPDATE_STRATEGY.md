# AlphaForge Data Update Strategy

## Purpose

Different data changes at different frequencies.

Each dataset should have a defined update schedule.

---

# Update Frequency

Real-Time

Examples:

- Prices
- Market Data

Daily

Examples:

- News
- Capital Flow

Quarterly

Examples:

- Financial Statements

Annual

Examples:

- Annual Reports
- ESG Reports

---

# Update Pipeline

Collect

↓

Validate

↓

Transform

↓

Store

↓

Verify

↓

Publish

---

# AlphaForge Principle

Fresh data improves decision quality only after verification.
