# AlphaForge Market Database

## Purpose

The Market Database stores structured information about financial markets around the world.

Its purpose is to provide market-level context before analyzing sectors, industries, and individual companies.

Markets influence capital flows, liquidity, valuation, and investor sentiment.

---

# Core Identity

Every market contains:

- Market ID
- Market Name
- Country
- Region
- Currency
- Time Zone
- Primary Exchange

---

# Market Characteristics

Store information about:

- Market Type
- Developed or Emerging
- Trading Hours
- Settlement Cycle
- Regulatory Authority

---

# Market Performance

Track:

- Major Indexes
- Historical Returns
- Volatility
- Market Breadth
- Liquidity

---

# Capital Flow

Track:

- Domestic Capital
- Foreign Capital
- ETF Flows
- Institutional Activity

---

# Economic Context

Related indicators:

- GDP Growth

- Interest Rates
- Exchange Rate
- Fiscal Policy
- Monetary Policy
