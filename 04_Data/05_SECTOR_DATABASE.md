# AlphaForge Sector Database

## Purpose

The Sector Database stores structured information about every economic sector.

Its purpose is to understand sector performance, capital rotation, macro sensitivity, and long-term investment opportunities.

Rather than simply classifying companies, the Sector Database explains why sectors outperform or underperform over time.

---

# Core Identity

Every sector contains:

- Sector ID
- Sector Name
- Description
- Parent Market

---

# Sector Characteristics

Store information about:

- Business Cycle
- Growth Characteristics
- Defensive or Cyclical
- Capital Intensity
- Innovation Level

---

# Macro Sensitivity

Each sector should be evaluated against:

- Interest Rates
- Inflation
- GDP Growth
- Oil Prices
- Currency
- Government Policy

---

# Performance Metrics

Track:

- Historical Returns
- Relative Strength
- Momentum
- Volatility
- Drawdown

---

# Capital Flow

Track:

- Institutional Buying
- Foreign Flow
- ETF Flow
- Smart Money Activity

---

# Industry Relationships

Each sector contains:

- Industries
- Leading Industries
- Emerging Industries

---

# Leading Companies

Store:

- Market Leaders
- Challengers
- High Growth Companies

---

# Risk Factors

Examples:

- Regulation
- Technology Disruption
- Commodity Prices
- Competition

---

# Metadata

Every record contains:

- Source
- Last Updated
- Version
- Verification Status

---

# AlphaForge Principle

Sectors lead industries.

Industries lead companies.

Understanding sector rotation helps identify opportunities before individual companies outperform.
