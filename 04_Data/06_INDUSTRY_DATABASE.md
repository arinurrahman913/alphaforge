# AlphaForge Industry Database

## Purpose

The Industry Database stores structured information about industries within each sector.

Its purpose is to understand industry dynamics, competitive positioning, structural growth, and long-term investment opportunities.

Industries serve as the bridge between sectors and individual companies.

---

# Core Identity

Every industry contains:

- Industry ID
- Industry Name
- Parent Sector
- Description

---

# Industry Characteristics

Store information about:

- Industry Lifecycle
- Market Size
- Growth Rate
- Profitability
- Capital Intensity

---

# Competitive Landscape

Track:

- Market Leaders
- Challengers
- Emerging Companies
- Market Concentration
- Barriers to Entry

# Industry Drivers

# Industry Metrics

# AlphaForge Principle

Industries determine where competitive advantages are created.

Understanding industry structure is essential before evaluating individual companies.
