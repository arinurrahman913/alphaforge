# AlphaForge Company Database

## Purpose

The Company Database stores all structured information related to publicly listed companies.

It acts as the central source of truth for every company inside AlphaForge.

Every company record must be complete, traceable, versioned, and connected to the AlphaForge Ontology.

---

# Core Identity

Every company must contain:

- Company ID
- Company Name
- Ticker Symbol
- Exchange
- Country
- Currency
- ISIN (optional)
- CIK (if available)

# AlphaForge Principle

A company is not just a ticker.

It is a living business connected to customers, competitors, management, financial performance, macroeconomic conditions, and long-term investment opportunities.
