# AlphaForge System Overview

## What is AlphaForge?

AlphaForge is an Investment Intelligence Operating System designed to improve long-term investment decision quality.

It combines structured knowledge, trusted data, disciplined research, decision frameworks, and AI into one integrated system.

AlphaForge does not exist to predict the future.

AlphaForge exists to improve how investment decisions are made.

---

# Mission

Build the world's most trusted Investment Intelligence Operating System.

---

# North Star

Help investors make better long-term investment decisions through transparent knowledge, structured reasoning, and explainable intelligence.

---

# Core Capabilities

AlphaForge is designed to:

- Understand businesses.
- Understand industries.
- Understand sectors.
- Understand macroeconomics.
- Build investment knowledge.
- Generate investment theses.
- Explain every conclusion.
- Continuously learn.
