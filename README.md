# AlphaForge

> **Building trusted investment intelligence for generations.**

---

# What is AlphaForge?

AlphaForge is an **Investment Intelligence Operating System (IIOS)** designed to help investors make better long-term investment decisions.

Unlike traditional investment platforms that primarily provide data, news, or analysis, AlphaForge integrates structured knowledge, trusted data, disciplined research, explainable reasoning, and responsible AI into a single decision intelligence system.

AlphaForge does not exist to predict the future.

AlphaForge exists to improve the quality of investment decisions.

---

# Why AlphaForge?

Modern investing is overwhelmed by information.

Financial statements.

Economic reports.

News.

Valuation metrics.

Research papers.

Alternative data.

Most platforms help investors access information.

AlphaForge helps investors transform information into structured knowledge, disciplined reasoning, and explainable investment decisions.

We believe better decisions come from better thinking—not better predictions.

# Architecture

AlphaForge is built as a layered knowledge architecture.

```text
Foundation
        ↓
Ontology
        ↓
Knowledge
        ↓
Research
        ↓
Data
        ↓
Playbooks
        ↓
Reasoning Engine
        ↓
AI
        ↓
Dashboard
```

Each layer builds upon the previous one to ensure transparency, scalability, maintainability, and explainability.

# AlphaForge v0.1 Goal

Generate one complete, professional-quality, explainable investment thesis for a single company.

The thesis should include:

* Company Overview
* Business Quality
* Industry Analysis
* Sector Analysis
* Financial Analysis
* Valuation
* Risks
* Catalysts
* Investment Thesis
* Confidence Score

**Progress toward this goal:** the Core Engine currently produces
Financial Analysis (scoring + snapshot) and basic Technical Analysis.
Business Quality, Industry/Sector Analysis, Valuation, Risks, Catalysts,
and a unified Investment Thesis are not yet implemented in code — they
exist only as playbook specifications in `05_Playbooks/`.
