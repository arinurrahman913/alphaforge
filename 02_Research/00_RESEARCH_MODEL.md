# AlphaForge Research Model

## Purpose

The AlphaForge Research Model defines how investment ideas are generated, validated, challenged, and documented.

Research transforms raw knowledge into actionable investment decisions.

---

## Research Philosophy

Knowledge answers:

"What is true?"

Research answers:

"What does it mean?"

Decision answers:

"What should we do?"

---

## Research Flow

Knowledge

↓

Evidence

↓

Analysis

↓

Investment Thesis

↓

Decision

↓

Review

↓

Learning

---

## Research Principles

- Every conclusion requires evidence.
- Every assumption must be testable.
- Separate facts from opinions.
- Document uncertainty.
- Challenge every thesis.
- Learn from every investment.

---

## Research Output

Every research project should produce:

- Investment Thesis
- Risks
- Opportunities
- Confidence Level
- Decision Recommendation
