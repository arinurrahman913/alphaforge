# AlphaForge Knowledge Model

## Purpose

The AlphaForge Knowledge Model defines how investment knowledge is organized, connected, and interpreted.

Rather than storing isolated information, AlphaForge builds relationships between every investment entity.

---

## Core Knowledge Layers

1. Market
2. Sector
3. Industry
4. Company
5. Financial Statements
6. Valuation
7. Economic Indicators
8. Portfolio
9. Investment Thesis
10. Risk Factors

---

## Knowledge Relationships

Market
└── Sector
      └── Industry
            └── Company
                  ├── Financial Data
                  ├── News
                  ├── Valuation
                  ├── Competitive Position
                  ├── Risks
                  └── Investment Thesis

---

## Design Principles

- Everything is connected.
- Every conclusion must be explainable.
- Evidence precedes opinion.
- Knowledge evolves continuously.
- Data supports reasoning, not replaces it.
