# AlphaForge Vision

---

## Vision

To become the world's most trusted investment intelligence platform.

---

## Long-Term Vision

We envision a future where every investment decision is supported by transparent knowledge, explainable analysis, reliable data, and responsible artificial intelligence.

AlphaForge should become a platform that investors trust not because it predicts the future, but because it improves the quality of thinking.

---

## Legacy

AlphaForge is designed to become a long-term knowledge asset.

The value of the platform should continue increasing as more research, data, and experience are accumulated.

Knowledge should outlive technology.

Technology should serve knowledge.
