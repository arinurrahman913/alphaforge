# AlphaForge Founding Charter

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 27 June 2026

---

# Purpose

AlphaForge exists to build trusted investment intelligence that helps people make better long-term investment decisions.

We believe that investing is not about predicting the future with certainty. It is about improving the quality of decisions by combining structured knowledge, disciplined research, reliable data, and responsible use of technology.

AlphaForge is built to become a long-term knowledge system rather than a short-term software project.

---

# Why AlphaForge Exists

Modern investors face an overwhelming amount of information every day.

Financial statements, earnings calls, economic reports, news, insider transactions, hedge fund filings, valuation metrics, and market sentiment are scattered across many different platforms.

Most tools provide access to information.

Very few help transform information into structured knowledge.

AlphaForge exists to bridge that gap.

Our goal is to organize knowledge in a way that helps investors understand businesses more deeply and make better long-term investment decisions.

---

# What AlphaForge Is

AlphaForge is an Investment Intelligence Operating System.

It is not simply:

- a stock screener
- a dashboard
- an AI chatbot
- a portfolio tracker

Those are only components.

The true product is a structured investment knowledge system.

---

# Our Philosophy

We believe that:

Knowledge compounds.

Good decisions compound.

Long-term thinking compounds.

Technology changes.

Knowledge remains.

For that reason, knowledge is treated as the primary asset of AlphaForge.

Software exists to organize, maintain, and deliver knowledge.

---

# Long-Term Commitment

Every document...

Every database...

Every playbook...

Every research note...

Every AI agent...

should increase the total knowledge of AlphaForge.

Nothing is built simply because it is technically interesting.

Everything must contribute to better investment decisions.

---

# Responsibility

AlphaForge is designed to assist human judgment.

It does not replace responsibility.

Every investment decision remains the responsibility of the investor.

The purpose of AlphaForge is to improve understanding, not to guarantee outcomes.

---

# Guiding Principle

Mission above founder.

Knowledge above software.

Truth above opinion.

Long-term above short-term.

Continuous improvement over perfection.
